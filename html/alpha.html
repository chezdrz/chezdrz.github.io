Alpha Reports
These reports are extracted based on the following criteria.
The 52 week high for each stock is taken.

An Alpha sell rate which is 5% below the 52week high is set
On this sell rate
The stock should have crossed the Alpha sell mark at least 2 times in the current year
The Profit margin from this Alpha sell rate should be min 8% or more

The times it crossed the sell rate is displayed
The Profit % based on the last day closing is displayed.
